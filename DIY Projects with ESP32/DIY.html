ESP32 Motor Control with PWM

In this DIY project I experimented with controlling a DC motor directly using an ESP32 and a simple NPN transistor 8050 without using a relay module.

I got the idea after watching a tutorial where a similar circuit was used to control a relay that switched the motor.

I wanted to test if the same principle could be used to control the motor directly through the transistor itself.

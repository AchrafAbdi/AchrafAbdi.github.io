GameCube Portable Controller Project

Development of a custom wireless GameCube controller with an integrated display, speakers and a 3D-printed ergonomic housing.

Back to Portfolio

Project Summary

This project focused on the development of a custom wireless GameCube controller with an integrated 7-inch display and speakers. The main technical focus was the design and construction of a custom 3D-printed housing that could hold all additional components while keeping the controller usable and ergonomic.

The housing was designed in Fusion 360 and divided into several individual parts for 3D printing. The electrical system combines a display, speakers, wireless HDMI transmission, a powerbank and a DC-DC step-down converter.

  • Custom 3D-printed controller housing made from PLA filament
  • Integrated 7-inch HDMI-compatible display
  • Integrated speakers for direct audio output
  • Wireless HDMI transmission for video and audio
  • Powerbank-based mobile power supply
  • Mechanical design split into multiple printable housing parts
